Tubulideres seminoli Sø rensen et al.,

Short description

with segment 1 consisting of a closed ring, segment 2 consisting of a midventrally articulated plate, and segments 3 to 11 of one tergal and two sternal plates.
Acicular spines present in middorsal series on segments 1 to 11, and in lateral series on segments 2, and 4 through 10.
Three pairs of small tubes are located in lateroventral and ventromedial positions on segment 2.
MTS, LTS and LTAS present.

Key characters and similar species

This monotypic genus accommodates theonly known kinorhynch species with segment 2 consisting of a single, midventrally articulating plate.

However, since segmental plate articulations might be difficult to visualise in species with thin cuticle, a better and safer diagnostic character is the three pairs of tubes in the lateral series of segment 2. Also this character trait is unique among kinorhynchs.

Distribution and habitat

The species is known from the Northwest Atlantic, 4-5 miles off Fort Pierce, Florida, and is found in clean shell hash on 15 m depth.
