Short description

Segment 1 consists of a closed cuticular ring, and segments 2 to 11 of one tergal and two sternal plates. Acicular spinesarepresent in middorsal series on segments 2 to 9, but those on segments 3 to 8 are alternatingly displaced to paradorsal positions.
Lateral series with tubes on segments 2, 5, and 8, and acicular spines on segments 6 to 9.
LTS arepresent, but LTAS and MTS absent.

Key characters and similar species

Species of Dracoderescan all be distinguished by their combinedpatterns of dorsal and lateralspines and tubes. Dracoderessnufkinidoes not show any single, unique characters, but is distinguished by this combined spine and tube pattern. It shows closest resemblance to D. abei, but is distinguished from this species by its absence of lateral tubeson segment 10.

Distribution and habitat

The specieswas described from muddy sand at 30 m depth near Okinawa, Japan. It has also been recorded from Iriomote Island, southwest of Okinawa.
