Long flexible ventrolateral acicular spines on segment 1. Tubes present in lateral series on segments 2 and 5.
Lateroventral acicular spines present on segments 8 and 9; segment 8 spines are short but robust, while segment 9 spines are longer and slenderer.
MTS, LTS and LTAS present.

Key characters and similar species

The speciescan be distinguished by the different appearances of lateral spines on segment 8 and 9: while the spines on segment 8 are shorter than those on segment 9, they are also very robust, and more than twice as thick. This trait is unique for the species.

Distribution and habitat

The species was described fromthe Black Sea, but has been recorded at numerous localitiesthroughout the Mediterranean and West European coast, inclusive Ireland and Sweden.
