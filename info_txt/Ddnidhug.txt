Short description

Segment 1 consists of a closed cuticular ring, and segments 2 to 11 of one tergal and two sternal plates.
Acicular spines arepresent in middorsal series on segments 3 to 9, but those on segments 4 to 8 are alternatingly displaced to paradorsal positions. Subdorsal tubes are present on segment 2. Lateral serieswith tubes on segments 2, 5, 8, and 10, and acicular spines on segments 6 to 9. LTSarepresent, but LTAS and MTS absent.

Species of Dracoderescan all be distinguished by their combinedpatterns of dorsal and lateralspines and tubes. Dracoderesnidhugis easily distinguished from all congeners, as this is the only species without dorsal acicular spine on segment 2, and instead with a pair of subdorsal tubes.

Distribution and habitat

The speciesis described from muddy sediments at 160 m deptheast of Uljin at the Korean east coast.
