Short description

moderate dorsal and ventral concavities along anterior margin of segment 1. Acicular spines present in middorsal series on segments 1 to 11, and in lateral series on segments 2 to 10. Well-developed cuspidate spines present in lateral series on segments 2, 5, 8 and 9 (cuspidate spine on segment 9 morelateral than corresponding acicular spine).
MTS, LTS and LTAS present.

Key characters and similar species

The species isdistinguished from A. incomitataby having a single pair, rather than two pairs, of cuspidate spines on segment 9. It furthermoredistinguished from allother congenersby its relative position ofcuspidate spines on segment 9, which attach more lateral than the corresponding acicular spines. In other congeners the segment 9 cuspidate spines attach more ventral.

Distribution and habitat

The species was described fromthe Fieberling Guyot seamountthe East Pacific, welloff Baja California Peninsula, and was found incalcareous sandbetween 500 and 700 m depth.
