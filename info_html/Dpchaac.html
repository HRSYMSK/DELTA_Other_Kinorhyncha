Short description

Segment 1 consists of a closed cuticular ring, and segments 2 to 11 of one tergal and two sternal plates. Acicular spinesarepresent in dorsal series on segments 1 to 9, butonly the spine on segment 1 is middorsal.
From segment 2, the dorsal spines are alternatingly, laterally displaced, to paradorsal positions on segment 2 and 3, and even more extreme to subdorsal positions on segments 4 to 9. Long, flexible ventrolateral spines are present on segment 1, and more regular looking acicular spines in lateral series on segments 2 to 9. Lateral tubes are present on segments 2 and 5. Lateroventral glandular cell outlets type 2 are present on segment 10. LTSare present, but LTAS and MTS absent.

Species of Dracophyesare distinguished by theposition of tubes in the lateralseries. Dracophyes chaacdiffers from D. cepedaiby having lateral accessory tubes on segments 2 and 5 only ( D. cepedaihas lateral tubes on segment 8 as well), and from D. toyoshioaeby lacking tubes on segments 2 to 3, and 6 to 7.

Distribution and habitat

The specieswas described from the southern part of the Gulf of Mexico, andlivesin muddy sedimentsat 200 m depth.
