Short description

Species with segment 1 consisting of a closed ring, segments 2 to 10 of one tergal and two sternal plates, and segment 11 of one tergal and one sternal plate. Acicular spines present in middorsal series on segments 1 to 9 (and segment 10 inclusive in stage 1 females), and lateral series on segments 1 to 9. Cuspidate spines are present in the lateral series on segments 1, 5, and 8.
MTS and LTAS present. LTAS longer than MTS.

Adult dimorphism: Adult dimorphism has beendescribed for this species. It is expressed in segment 10, where stage 1 females have thin, acicular spines in middorsal and laterodorsal positions, whereas spineson segment 10 are absent in stage 2 females. Also males appear to lack spines on segment 10. Adult dimorphism in males has not yet been observed.

Key characters and similar species
