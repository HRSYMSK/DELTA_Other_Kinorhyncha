Long flexible ventrolateral acicular spines on segment 1. Tubes present in lateral series on segments 2, 5 and 7.
Lateroventral acicular spines present on segments 8 and 9; spines on segment 9 are only slightly longer than those on segment 8, but they are more than twice as thick at their bases.
MTS, LTS and LTAS present.

Key characters and similar species

The speciesis most easily distinguished by the presence of paired lateroventral tubes on segment 7 – this character is only shared with C. bonnyae.
However, in addition, C. bonnyae also has lateroventral tubes on segment 8. Such tubes are not present in C. barbanigra.

Distribution and habitat

The species was described from the Gulf of Mexico, and has been reported from numerous localities throughout the Gulf of Mexico and Caribbean Sea, as well as from Panama and Bermuda.
