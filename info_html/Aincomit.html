Short description

moderatedorsal and ventral concavities along anterior margin of segment 1. Acicular spines present in middorsal series on segments 1 to 11, and in lateral series on segments 2 to 10. Well-developed cuspidate spines present in lateral series on segments 2, 5, 6, 8 (two pairs!)
and 9 (cuspidate spine on segment 9 more ventral than corresponding acicular spine).
MTS, LTS and LTAS present.
LTS:LTAS ratio nearly equal, or LTAS slightly longer than LTS.

Key characters and similar species

Thespecies is easily distinguished from other congeners, as well as all other known kinorhynch species, by its double set of cuspidate spines on segment 8.

Distribution and habitat

This Mediterranean species was described from the Northern Adriatic Sea, off Rovinj, Croatia, andwas found inmedium-grainedsand mud on 4 m depth. It has furthermore been recorded in southern Italy, near Taranto, in the Ligurian Sea in northwest Italy, and in Spain, off Barcelona.
