Short description

Species with segment 1 consisting of a closed ring, segments 2 to 10 of one tergal and two sternal plates, and segment 11 of one tergal and one sternal plate. Acicular spines present in middorsal series on segments 1 to 10 in males and segments 1 to 9 in females. In the lateral series acicular spines are presenton segments 1 to 9. Cuspidate spines are present in the lateral series on segments 5 and 9.
MTS and LTAS present. LTAS longer than MTS.

Adult dimorphism: Adult dimorphism is likely to occur in all species of Condyloderes. It is still not fully understood for all species, but it appears thatin particular occurrenceof acicular spines on segment 10 might differ between stages and sexes, thus researchers should be cautious about relying too much on segment 10 morphology during identification.
